About Resume Screening AI

An intelligent, AI-powered system designed to help recruiters and HR teams quickly identify the most relevant resumes for a given job role.

📄 Project Purpose

Companies receive hundreds or even thousands of resumes for a single job. Manual screening is slow, error-prone, and time-consuming.

Resume Screening AI automates this process by ranking resumes based on how closely they match the job description — saving time, effort, and hiring cost.

How AI Works

This system uses Natural Language Processing (NLP) techniques instead of complex model training.

Text Extraction

Text is extracted from resumes and job descriptions (PDF / TXT format).

NLP Processing

Text is cleaned, normalized, and converted into numerical vectors using TF-IDF.

Similarity Scoring

Cosine similarity calculates how closely each resume matches the job description.
